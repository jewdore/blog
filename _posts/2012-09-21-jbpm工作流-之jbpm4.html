jbpm-4.4.zip

mysql

导入 jbpm-4.4\install\src\db\create\ jbpm.mysql.create.sql

将apache-tomcat-6.0.26.zip 拷贝到jbpm-4.4\install\downloads\apache-tomcat-6.0.26.zip

jbpm-4.4\install\ant -Ddatabase=mysql demo.setup.tomcat

其中jbpm-4.4\install\src\demo\ example.identities.sql

使用

jbpm-4.4\apache-tomcat-6.0.26\bin\startup.bat
