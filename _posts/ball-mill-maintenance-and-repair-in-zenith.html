The following will introduce some tips to maintain ball mill.

1. When the ball mill has worked about a month, all lubrication oil should be released and replaced by new oil. Then ball mill should change oil every six months again.

2. Lubrication of every lubrication point and oil level should be checked at least once every 4 hours.

3. When ball mill is working, oil temperature of main bearing should not exceed 55 ℃.

4. Drive bearing and reducer's temperature does not exceed 55 ℃, the maximum does not exceed 60 ℃.

5. Large and small gears are working smoothly, there is no abnormal noise. When necessary, you should adjust clearance.

6. The ball mill runs smoothly, there is no strong vibration.

7. There should be no abnormal fluctuations in motor current.

8. Each connecting fasteners is not loose, there is no leaking oil, water and ore.

9. According to wear, steel ball should be added timely.

10. If finding an abnormal situation, you should immediately stop grinding to overhaul.

11. If ball mill liner is worn 70% or 70mm long cracks, they should be replaced in time.
